About Me

I am a PostDoc Researcher in the Department of Electronic Engineering at Tsinghua University, where I am affiliated with the Future Intelligence Laboratory (FIB-Lab). I got my Ph.D. in computer science and engineering from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in 2021, and received the B.S. degree and M.S. degree in communication engineering from Hunan University, China, in 2014 and 2017, respectively.

My research interests include ubiquitous computing, data science, and especially with applications to spatiotemporal data mining.
